# CREATIVE-DESIGN-RULES.md ,  static ad DESIGN engine (all brands)
> Path: `Statics/_FOUNDATION/CREATIVE-DESIGN-RULES.md` · GLOBAL ENGINE
> Distilled from GRANNY-STATICS-GUIDE §5 + Knigi STYLE-CONCEPT §3C (canonical, client-approved). Read before EVERY generation batch.
> Hard lesson: the design must vary as hard as the copy. Shipping a batch where every image is "headline in a band on top" = automatic fail (Axis-5 sameness).

## THE FAILURE TO NEVER REPEAT
Every creative with the same layout (same placement, same emphasis, same structure) = look-alike batch, even with different pictures. Catchy + pattern-interrupt comes from DESIGN, not only words.

## 1. HEADLINE DOMINATES ,  35-50% of the frame
Bold, big, unmissable, two-tier size hierarchy. Never a thin strip of small text. The accent REPLACES the normal-size word (never print a word twice / no echoed text).

## 2. ROTATE PLACEMENT (Axis 5 ,  #1 cause of sameness; never two adjacent the same)
giant caps top-dominant · centered over the object · bottom caption band · split (half top / half bottom, object between) · word-per-line stack (each word huge) · big number + small support · text-on-object as a clean overlay (sign / notebook / screen) · diagonal sticker/stamp · explainer callout bubbles pointing at parts of the scene.
> Nano garbles text "painted onto" objects/wrapped/vertical ,  keep text as a clean rendered overlay/band/caps block. Only reliably-integrated form = explainer callout bubbles. NEVER vertical/rotated text.
> **NEVER split a single word across two text zones** (test glitch: подготвено -> ПОДГОТВНО, нейното -> НЕЙ НОТО). Each word whole inside one zone. Longest headlines still risk a dropped letter even on Pro 2K, always verify finalists and regen the glitched. NEVER shorten the headline to fit the design, resize/relayout to hold the full headline (Knigi 3C).

## 3. ROTATE EMPHASIS DEVICE (every creative, different than neighbor)
marker-highlight behind a word · hand-drawn circle · underline scribble · one word huge contrast size · word in different font · strikethrough negative word + replacement · word in explosion/bubble · arrow to a word · different color / knockout in colored bar. Min 1 accented word.

## 4. FIVE DIVERSITY AXES ,  no two adjacent equal on ANY
(1) visual-type [illustration/3D/graphic/infographic/photo-native] (2) typography (3) illustration-style/"раса" (4) concept-type [literal/metaphor/stat/before-after/diagram] (5) **text-treatment & placement**.

## 5. LENGTH → FORMAT
Short headline (≤6 words) → GIANT caps, 40-50%. Long headline → split into a small setup line + a HUGE punch line, or a band. Never a 4-line uniform paragraph top-left. Never cut the headline to fit the design; change the layout to fit the headline.

## 6. ALWAYS
Catchy / pattern-interrupt. Visual SHOWS the headline (congruent, not decor). Text horizontal, Bulgarian only, no small labels/annotations/legends (they glitch to Latin), 0 em dash, no final period. Used-log per brand/cycle to guarantee zero repetition.

## 7. PROMPT must encode the design, not just the headline
Per creative, the prompt explicitly states: the placement (from §2), the emphasis device (from §3), the size dominance (§1), and the style ,  and each differs from the previous creative in the batch. A prompt that only gives the scene + "headline on top" reproduces the failure.

## 8. THE VISUAL IS THE FACE, TREATED STRIKINGLY ,  NO ABSTRACT METAPHOR OBJECTS (client-approved 2026-06)
The subject is ALWAYS the face / the woman, rendered in a catchy, conceptual way. Not a backdrop for a headline, and NOT an abstract object standing in for the idea.
FORBIDDEN (client, hard): abstract metaphor visuals ,  slot machine, trees, clock, scales, keys, any object-as-symbol. Too figurative, banned.
WINNING (client-approved):
- a hyperreal 3D face model split into living skin vs cracked porcelain/wax mask (APPROVED);
- a fine-art / classical PAINTING of a face with a conceptual intervention, e.g. a gloved hand varnishing a cracked oil portrait (APPROVED);
- a 3D face whose smooth surface cracks, hollows or caves in; a waxy/frozen face; a striking close-up where the mouth smiles but the eyes are dead; the same face progressively more frozen.
Realistic photo / 3D allowed ONLY if genuinely catchy/conceptual (an interesting 3D face model, never a stock portrait + headline).
RECIPE: take the truth about what botox does to the FACE → show it ON a face, treated strikingly (3D model, hyperreal, split, cracked, painted) → headline in a band, one word size-emphasis, small CTA button. Every creative a different render/treatment; serious grown-up palette (charcoal, oxblood, plum, teal, gold, cream, slate).

## 9. VARY THE SHOT ,  centered frontal face is THE TRAP (client-flagged 2026-06)
Face-centric does NOT mean the same centered, frontal, head-on portrait every time. A batch of centered frontal faces looks like one photo recolored (client caught exactly this). VARY every creative:
- ANGLE: frontal / profile / 3-4 / from below / from above / turned away
- CROP: full face / extreme close-up of eyes only / mouth only / half face / face + hands / head-and-shoulders / face filling only part of the frame
- POSE: hands touching or covering the face, looking away, caught candid, looking down, mid-expression
- VIEWPOINT: in a mirror, over the shoulder, a reflection, face partly out of frame, off-center on a rule-of-thirds
Never two adjacent the same composition. Centered-frontal is the default trap ,  at most ONCE per batch.

## 10. HEADLINE ↔ VISUAL SYNC + HEADLINE STAYS 30-40% (client-flagged 2026-06, top priority ,  overrides composition cleverness)
A) THE HEADLINE STAYS 30-40% OF THE FRAME, big and dominant. Do NOT shrink it into a side column to fit a fancy composition (the profile shots failed this and stopped being catchy). Vary the shot, but the headline still dominates and grabs.
B) PERFECT CONGRUENCE: the visual must LITERALLY depict what the headline says, so words and picture amplify each other and transmit a stronger feeling together. List the headline's concrete nouns and SHOW them:
   - „Спре ли иглата, бръчките се връщат по-дълбоки" → MUST show the needle AND the after-state (visibly deeper wrinkles). A face with no needle and no deeper-wrinkles = fail.
   - „бузите спадат" → show sagging cheeks · „очите не се усмихват" → smiling mouth + flat dead eyes in one shot · „след пет години" → a face with the cumulative frozen look · „десетата инжекция" → many injection marks + a worse-looking face.
If the picture doesn't show the headline's claim, it's wrong no matter how pretty. Catchy = the visual hits the SAME point the words do, harder.

## 11. VARY THE TYPE / FORMAT / WORLD ,  not just the art style on a face (client-flagged 2026-06)
A batch of "a face in 5 art styles" is still mono-type. Look at the books: EVERY creative is a different WORLD (kid covering ears / child sinking in ocean / pixel game / storm cloud / train platform). For Eterna, rotate the FORMAT/SUBJECT on every creative, mixing freely (realistic IS allowed ,  the client never banned it, only sameness):
testimonial card · lifestyle SCENE with a person (mirror, clinic waiting room, dinner, grandchild) · 3D sculpture/model of a face · science cutaway diagram · split / two faces · extreme macro · pop-art / bold graphic · classical painting · bold typographic poster · before/after-style.
No two creatives the same FORMAT in one batch. Still bound by: §8 (no abstract-symbol objects ,  slot/trees/clock), §10 (headline 30-40% + visual↔headline congruence), §9 (vary the shot).

## 12. WHAT WINNING COMPETITOR STATICS ACTUALLY LOOK LIKE (decoded from GetHookd, 2026-06)

They lead with (a) the PRODUCT made gorgeous, (b) native-content UGC before/after, or (c) ONE dramatic conceptual face. The sad/old face appears only as a quick contrast, never the whole mood.

The 9 winning FORMATS (each a different world ,  rotate across a batch):

3. **Premium RANGE HERO** ,  jars stacked on marble, bold headline („Rebuild your skin in days / No needle required") + buy button.
4. **Elegant TESTIMONIAL card** ,  5 gold stars + serif quote + a hand holding the open jar, cream whitespace.
5. **Minimalist TYPOGRAPHIC poster** ,  „One Product. One Minute. One Ritual." huge serif, massive negative space, product small.
6. **Product-in-action MACRO + bold claim** ,  finger dipping into the glowing jar, „Nature's BOTOX" (BOTOX huge).
7. **Dreamy ENCHANTED brand-world** ,  jar with glowing aurora + nordic forest + crescent moon, navy starry, reflection („One layer at night. Different skin by morning.").

Recurring engine: the product is the catchy HERO object (glowing, in a hand, dipped, stacked, made magical). Typography premium + varied (bold condensed sans + elegant serif + script accent), NEVER one band-on-top. Curiosity/mystery in nearly every headline.

ETERNA APPLICATION: marry these formats with the approved botox-lie headlines. Make the Eterna serum/drop a gorgeous hero object (golden drop / dropper, glowing). SPLIT FACE (§8 alive vs waxy-frozen) for fear/contrast headlines; „Nature's Botox"/„no needle" MACRO for product-positive; EDITORIAL LISTICLE for advertorial congruence (the destination IS a listicle). Palette may be premium-warm (cream/amber/marble/gold), not only grim-dark ,  aspirational beats depressing. Still bound by §8/§9/§10/§11.

## 13. THE BOOK METHOD IS THE DIVERSITY MODEL ,  CLIENT-LOCKED 2026-06-15 (overrides §12 Froya product shots)
Client verdict on the Froya-modeled batch: the PRODUCT / commerce shots (bottle on marble, dropper, enchanted brand-world, testimonial card, typographic poster) are REJECTED for Eterna ,  „абстрахирай се от Фроя". The real diversity model is the Ina Brands BOOK creatives (`~/Desktop/InaBrands/.../04_Static_Ads/vikane-batch-new` + Knigi STYLE-CONCEPT §3B). Each book creative is a completely different WORLD (music UPA cartoon · chemistry riso · ocean gouache · pixel game · weather flat-vector · train vintage-poster).

THE METHOD: take ONE emotional truth → throw it into a RANDOM life-domain (music, chemistry, ocean, video-game, weather, train, wax/candle, treadmill/cycle, art-restoration, ice/freezing, anatomy, theatre-mask, staircase, balloon, textile, botany, astronomy, plumbing, origami…) → find a TIGHTLY CONGRUENT visual metaphor INSIDE that domain → render in a DIFFERENT illustration style every time → dominant headline 35-50% → ONE accented word with a DIFFERENT device each time (red word · hand-drawn circle · underline · strikethrough · marker-highlight · huge size · arrow) → small CTA pill → the headline appears ONCE only (duplicate = bug) → all text horizontal, BG only, ZERO Latin anywhere in the image.

NOT photo by default (illustrated worlds). EXCEPTION for Eterna: conceptual PHOTO face-metaphors are allowed as ONE world among the illustrated ones ,  client-approved photo hits: the грациозно↔сковано SPLIT FACE, and the stop-the-needle BEFORE/AFTER deeper-wrinkles.

STYLE PALETTE for Eterna (women ~50, sophisticated, NEVER childish pixel/kawaii): riso/screenprint · mid-century satirical poster · fine-art classical oil · storybook gouache editorial · vintage propaganda/travel poster (halftone, clean white bg) · anatomical plate / flat medical infographic · art-deco · paper-cut collage · silhouette minimalist · woodblock · Bauhaus geometric · charcoal noir · botanical plate. USED-LOG (domain × style × accent) per cycle in `Eterna/eterna-botox-concepts-v1.md` ,  no two creatives share a domain or a style.

CLIENT-APPROVED DIRECTION TESTS (2026-06-15, match this quality): wax-candle face (riso) · endless-cycle hamster-wheel (mid-century satire) · varnished cracked oil portrait (fine-art). Headlines = the approved botox-lie set (book voice, 2nd person); do not let quality drop when scaling.

FORBIDDEN STYLES (client 2026-06-15): NO „cowboy" / western / wanted-poster look · NO yellowed / aged / sepia old-paper backgrounds · NO rustic western serif font (this killed the woodblock-on-aged-paper „Срещала си жена с гладко чело" test). Any vintage/print/linocut style ONLY on a CLEAN light/white background with a modern font. (Mirrors Granny rule: vintage on clean white, never aged/sepia.)

CLARITY LOCK (client 2026-06-15): every woman shown must read CLEARLY as 50+ via her FACE (real age on cheeks/neck/eyes), but with **well-kept, coloured & styled hair , NOT grey/silver** (a put-together woman who cares for herself, not a grey granny). AND the botox must be VISUALLY OBVIOUS at a glance: an unnaturally shiny, taut, frozen, slightly overfilled forehead + waxy immobile upper face , the unmistakable „overdone/done" look. The tell is NEVER subtle (subtle = the repeated fail). Best device = contrast: a clearly aged 50+ face (lower face/neck show age) whose forehead is frozen-smooth and shiny. „Да е клиър."

## 14. CREATIVE FORMAT PER AWARENESS (client-locked 2026-06-15)
The creative format follows the awareness state, NOT one look for all:
- **Unaware / Problem-aware → illustrated conceptual „different worlds"** (the book method §13): metaphor-in-a-domain, varied art styles, the fear/dread, dominant headline. NO product needed.
- **Solution-aware → CLEAN, PREMIUM, PRODUCT-FORWARD** , modelled on the client refs (DRMTLGY, gisou): light/cream background, big bold modern sans headline (top-left), the product shown clean, a BENEFIT CHECKLIST with check icons (or labelled callouts pointing at the product), optional price badge. Minimal, commercial, light palette. This is the ONLY place product-forward is wanted (it is NOT the banned vague Froya brand-world , here it's bold-headline + clear benefits + product).
- **Product / Most-aware → product + offer/proof** (price, guarantee, reviews 4.77/234 Judge.me), same clean commercial look.
NOTE: solution-aware/product creatives need the REAL Eterna Drop product photo for final; until provided, use a clean unbranded premium dropper as placeholder and flag it.

## 15. MATRIX VERDICTS + THE CONGRUENCE/EMOTION LAW (client review 2026-06-15)
THE LAW (overrides cleverness): the visual must VISUALIZE, SUPPORT and AMPLIFY the headline's concept AND its emotion, and DELIVER its payoff.
- If the headline implies she looks bad/frozen at 65 → SHOW her frozen/worse at 65. (The „45→65" render FAILED because at 65 she still looked good = contradicts the copy. The picture must deliver the claim.)
- RIGHT EMOTION: a fear/loss headline must NOT show a happy face (mid-century happy woman = fail).
- TIE TO BOTOX: a generically nice scene with no link to the botox message = fail (the fine-art happy grandma had zero botox connection).
- ZERO text errors, ZERO duplicated words/letters (hard, re-emphasized).
- Headline must be BIG/dominant even in infographic layouts (the brochure one had too-small headline).

KEEP / AMPLIFY (client-loved): Bold **3D CGI conceptual render** ★★ · Refined **charcoal & graphite editorial** on clean white ★★ · Editorial **PHOTOREAL before/after portrait** (stacked 50 vs 65) ★★ · **Documentary photo grid** ★ · **Minimal line-art + speech bubble** ★ (vary colours/styles) · **Pop-art comic halftone** ✓ · **Surreal Magritte** ✓ (FIX the headline treatment) · **Conceptual split** ✓ (strengthen the effect) · **Storybook gouache** ✓ (tentative) · **Paper-cut collage** ◐ (marginal) · **Infographic/brochure** ✓ (headline must be BIG).

KILL (never use again for Eterna): **Isometric 3D miniature diorama** (text unreadable) · **Native/UGC candid** (not catchy) · **Fine-art classical oil „happy grandma"** execution (no botox link) · **Mid-century modern** (headline doesn't pop + wrong happy emotion) · **Claymation / 3D clay** (removed entirely).

## 16. IDEA-PATTERN BANK (competitor FEED, 2026-06-16) , inspiration, NOT a template to copy
From a top weight-loss/cortisol advertiser's highest-impression ads (different niche; steal the PATTERN, not the execution). These kill „boring":
1. **Red TAG/badge above the headline** („КОРТИЗОЛ ДЕТОКС", „НОВ ДОКЛАД") → instant news/report look. For Eterna: „БОТОКС ИСТИНАТА" · „НОВ ДОКЛАД" · „ДЕРМАТОЛОГ РАЗКРИВА".
2. **Hyperreal anatomy / micro-world** (organs, cells, bloodstream) , visceral, thumb-stopping. For Eterna: the FROZEN MUSCLE under the skin · the toxin spreading under the skin · an atrophied muscle fibre · facial-anatomy cutaway.
3. **Anatomy diagram + callout circles + red-X symptom checklist** (two-figure educational). For Eterna: two faces (front/profile) with callouts to each tell (увиснала вежда, спаднали бузи, восъчно чело) + red-X list of side-effects.
4. **Authority / study framing** („Специалист разкрива 5-те причини", „47 жени, среден резултат -6кг") , looks like content (advertorial-congruent). For Eterna: „Дерматолог разкрива 5-те…", a study/report angle.
5. **Hand-drawn notebook/explainer** (graph + sketch + red arrows) on CLEAN white/lined paper (NOT aged/sepia) , doctor's-note authenticity.
6. **Fine-art emotion + serif + reframe** , WORKS only when the painted emotion ties to the problem (despair/frustration), unlike the killed „happy grandma".
7. Consistent **„ПРОЧЕТИ ПОВЕЧЕ →"** arrow CTA for advertorial click.
Apply these to the approved LF8 botox headlines to lift the duller illustrated faces.

## 17. EXECUTION, NOT JUST THE IDEA (client 2026-06-16: „не идеята, а самият визуал и как е направен")
A congruent concept rendered UGLY still fails. The visual EXECUTION must be polished, professional and appealing. FORBIDDEN ugly/morbid/gross renders (client called them „гадни"): grey death-mask / tombstone marble busts · melting wax busts with drips · raw-meat / muscle-fibre macro (looks like meat) · skull x-rays · blank creepy mannequin/mask faces · anything death-adjacent or repulsive. Catchy ≠ ugly/morbid. KEEP these well-crafted, appealing executions: clean editorial photo (report „НОВ ДОКЛАД"), pop-art comic, woodcut/linocut on white, notebook explainer, clean medical callout diagram, refined illustration. When a strong IDEA (e.g. „лицето като восък/камък") renders morbid, re-do it in a CLEAN, elegant execution (e.g. a refined editorial portrait with a subtle waxy sheen), not a grim 3D sculpture.

## 18. LAYOUT , FULL-BLEED INTEGRATED, never the „Indian presentation" split (client 2026-06-16)
The image must be FULL-BLEED (fills the whole frame) and the headline INTEGRATED as an overlay ON it , ONE unified composition, like the competitor feed (anatomy/organ full-bleed + dark gradient at the bottom + red tag + headline over the image). FORBIDDEN: a separate headline text-block stacked ABOVE/BELOW a small contained photo , that reads like a cheap „индийска презентация"/PowerPoint slide (the marble #1 and the report „НОВ ДОКЛАД"+photo #6 failed exactly this). For legibility put a subtle scrim/gradient or a thin color band UNDER the text, but the photo still fills the frame edge-to-edge. Integrated layouts that WORK: pop-art (text inside the art), notebook (text is the page), full-bleed photo/anatomy/micro-world with headline overlaid, callout diagram. So even „НОВ ДОКЛАД/Дерматолог разкрива" must be full-bleed (clinic photo edge-to-edge + bottom gradient + red tag + headline on it), NOT a white caption block above a framed photo.
 VARY THE INTEGRATION DEVICE per creative (client 2026-06-16): red tag + bottom gradient is ONE option, NOT a formula for every creative , rotate the treatment (sometimes a tag, sometimes none; different accent / placement / scrim style / no band). Full-bleed + integrated + scroll-stopping is the constant; the specific device rotates (Axis 5).


## 19. ETHNICITY + „NO STOCK-PORTRAIT" (client 2026-06-16, two hard fails on the „восък" render)
A) PEOPLE: ONLY European / Bulgarian women (and people). NEVER Black, Asian or other ethnicities. Hair COLOURED & styled, NOT grey. (The render that failed: a Black, grey-haired woman , two violations.)
B) FORBIDDEN FORMAT , the dumbest, most boring one: a plain STOCK-PHOTO PORTRAIT (a woman posing / looking at camera) + a text band. „тъпа сток снимка със скучен хедлайн" = automatic fail, even full-bleed. A stock person + caption does NOT stop the scroll. The visual must be a genuinely INTERESTING CONCEPT (pop-art, notebook explainer, callout diagram, clean anatomy / micro-world, conceptual illustration). If a real woman appears she must be INSIDE an interesting concept/scene (not a posed portrait), European, coloured hair, with the visible botox tell. Scroll-stop comes from the CONCEPT, never from a stock face.
