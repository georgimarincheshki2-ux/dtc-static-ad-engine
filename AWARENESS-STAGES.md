## 5. WHAT EACH STATE MUST DO (Eterna, Stage 3-4, BG, 3rd person) — weak vs strong

### State 5 — UNAWARE (Story/Curiosity · Secret/Story lead · Urgent NOT scored)
- Job: name a moment she recognizes; create the problem. No product/category/mechanism.
- WEAK: „Ботоксът не е единственото решение" (leads with enemy).
- STRONG: „Жените над 50 го забелязват първо на врата и по ръцете, не на лицето" (3rd person, specific moment, opens loop). [This is the C001 #10 that was wrongly benched — it is a model winner.]
- STRONG (identity): „Има възраст, на която снимките, направени неподготвено, спират да харесват." (3rd person rewrite of C001 #11.)

### State 4 — PROBLEM-AWARE (Question/Problem · Story lead · Urgent NOT scored) — main cold state
- Job: prove the problem is understood better than she can say it; agitate a specific moment (Hopkins Doc 15: named culprit > abstraction, hope > pure fear).
- WEAK: „Бръчките са нормална част от стареенето" (flat).
- STRONG (mechanism-pointing): „Дълбоките бръчки не състаряват най-много. Състаряват онези, които се появяват само при усмивка." (3rd person, precise culprit, opens loop toward mechanism without naming it.)
- STRONG: „Кремът спира на повърхността. Бръчката се образува милиметри по-надолу." (3rd person, surface-vs-depth, closed thought.)
- Mechanism note: the mechanism is the PAYOFF inside the advertorial, not the headline. The headline POINTS at it and opens the loop.

### State 3 — SOLUTION-AWARE (News/How-to · Problem-Solution lead · Urgent floored ≤5)
- Job: position the TYPE of solution as smarter. At Stage 3-4 → lead with the mechanism as a new way vs needles.
- WEAK: „Нашият крем действа по-добре от другите" (brag, no mechanism).
- STRONG: „Все повече жени над 50 избират капка сутрин вместо игла на всеки три месеца." (3rd person — note: this is bandwagon/AUTHORITY, tag it correctly, see §6.)
- STRONG (mechanism contrast): „Иглата изпъва кожата отгоре. Има грижа, която работи там, където тя не стига." (3rd person.)

### State 2 — PRODUCT-AWARE (USP/Differentiation · Promise lead · full 4U incl. Urgent)
- Job: close the gap from "seen it" to "it's the one." Differentiator or proof.
- WEAK: „Eterna Drop — красива кожа всеки ден" (vague benefit, dead at Stage 3-4).
- STRONG: „Eterna Drop изглажда мимическите бръчки за 28 дни — или връщаме парите." (named outcome + timeframe + guarantee.)

### State 1 — MOST AWARE (Pure Offer · Offer lead · full 4U incl. Urgent)
- Job: deliver offer/deadline. Shortest. CTA only on closing headline.
- STRONG: „-30% на серията Eterna Drop само до неделя."

### GUARD 1 — SPREAD CHECK (anti-gaming, new):
If the WHOLE batch lands within 2 points of the cut line on either gate (e.g. everything at 25/40 and 11-12/15), the batch was written to the test, not to the woman. **Reject and regenerate.** A real batch has spread: some 30s+, some kills. A flat wall at the floor = gamed = automatic reject.

### GUARD 2 — IDEA-DEDUP (not text-dedup, new):
"Zero duplicate text" is not enough. Check duplicate IDEA. C001 shipped #1/#2/#3 all = surface-vs-depth mechanism (3 ideas wearing different shirts). Max 2 headlines per distinct idea per batch. If 3+ share a concept, keep the best and regenerate the rest on a different angle.

Data override: when WINNERS-FEEDBACK.md contradicts a score, the data wins (recalibrate, don't argue). FRESHNESS (2026-07-02): only a FRESH snapshot counts (pull ≤14 days old, period-based). Stale winners are history , inspiration allowed, blind replication forbidden; „what's winning" ALWAYS triggers a new Meta pull for a recent window.
