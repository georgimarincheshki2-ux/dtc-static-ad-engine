# HEADLINE-ENGINE.md , the reusable formula for catchy, specific headlines (ANY brand / angle / lander)
> GLOBAL. Whatever angle, lander, or product the user sends, run EVERY headline through this. Distilled from the Eterna botox-lie work the client repeatedly approved as „кечи, хитваме целта, специфичност, пърфект" (2026-06-15). Pairs with Copy[OS] Doc 03 (awareness) + Doc 07 (4U/curiosity) + Doc 15 (Hopkins).

## STEP 0 , DIAGNOSE (before writing)
- Awareness STATE (Schwartz, Doc 03): 5 Unaware · 4 Problem-aware · 3 Solution-aware · 2 Product-aware · 1 Most-aware. Colder state = further from product/enemy; warmer = more direct + proof/offer.
- Sophistication stage → if the market is tired of plain claims, lead with mechanism-reframe or identity, never a naked benefit.

## STEP 1 , HOPKINS 6-PART pre-write (every headline)
Named culprit (visible, not abstract) · consequence (social/emotional) · daily trigger (a real moment) · instant reward · real VOC wording · theme. Principles: named villain > abstraction · hope > pure fear · trigger + reward = the hook.

## STEP 2 , THE 5 NON-NEGOTIABLES (this is what makes them land)
1. **ONE concrete LIVED MOMENT / observation** , a specific scene she recognizes, never an abstract generality. (cafe foreheads · the daylight photo · the 10th injection · 45→65)
2. **ULTRA-SPECIFIC** , concrete nouns + real numbers/details. Specificity = believability + grip. Vague = dead.
3. **AIRTIGHT 2-PART LOGIC** , setup + punch that LOGICALLY connect, and the punch LANDS with stakes. Never 3 disconnected states. Weak/soft endings kill it.
4. **THE RIGHT WORDS** , say the thing directly; no dodging into vague adjectives („меко и живо"); use the precise VOC word. Banned weak/wrong words (e.g. for a frozen face: NOT „замръзнало/застинало" → use вкочанено/сковано/неподвижно/восъчно/атрофирало or „не помръдва").
5. **TENSION MATCHES AWARENESS:**
   - **Unaware** → curiosity + FUTURE-UNKNOWN dread („как ще изглежда това на 65?"), enter through a collective truth/observation, the solution stays a mystery. Tight, one thread, strong landing.
   - **Problem-aware** → NAME the problem she already feels; agitate the exact daily moment.
   - **Solution-aware** → contrast the two solution TYPES, position the smarter one, outcome-led (needle vs the daily care).
   - **Product/Most-aware** → differentiator + proof, then offer/deadline.

## STEP 3 , VOICE & MECHANICS
Book voice (2nd person where natural), closed thought, finish the second beat. No taxonomy/lecture opener („X са два вида"). Max ~18-20 words; long → small setup line + big punch. Grammar: „се" before the verb · пълен/кратък член · „кавички" долни-горни · 0 em dash · no final period · real numbers only (flag unsourced [verify]).

CTA , OPTIONAL & SELECTIVE (client 2026-06-16): NOT on every creative. Add a CTA ONLY when the headline leaves a genuine OPEN LOOP that the CTA resolves (e.g. „…Тогава ще е късно" → „Виж какво да направиш сега"; „Тайната почти никога не е ботокс" → „Виж тайната"). A COMPLETED thought needs NO CTA. The CTA must be SPECIFIC and point at a concrete thing , never vague („Виж разликата" = difference with WHAT? = fail).

CLARITY OF THE FIRST HALF (client 2026-06-16): the SETUP (first part) of the headline must be clear and CONVINCING on its own , no cryptic openers („със спринцовката има само една посока, напред" = unclear what „напред" means = fail). Both halves land; the setup especially must be instantly understood.

NEEDLE/BOTOX SYNONYM ROTATION (client 2026-06-16): do NOT repeat „иглата" every line. Rotate: инжекцията/инжекциите · ботоксът · спринцовката · убождането/боцкането/„да се бодеш" · процедурите · дозата · ампулата · „кабинетът"/„часът при естетика" (metonym) · „часът на всеки три месеца" (the cycle) · токсинът (bold/accurate , botulinum toxin; careful on Meta).

## STEP 4 , KILL-TESTS (any NO = rewrite)
1. Does a cold reader get it in 1 second WITHOUT the lander?
2. Is there a concrete picture I could draw? (congruence-ready)
3. Do the two halves connect with airtight logic AND a landing punch?
4. Is every word the RIGHT word (no vague, no banned)?
5. Does the tension match the awareness state?
6. Is it ultra-specific (a number / scene / concrete detail)?

## STEP 5 , SPREAD & DEDUP (per batch)
Rotate FORM: story · question · number · secret · reframe · two-state contrast · lived moment. Max 2 headlines per distinct IDEA. No flat wall; real spread of strength.

## STEP 5B , THE HEADLINE MATRIX (idea-dedup at the ANGLE level , learned 2026-06-15)
THE TRAP: a batch can reuse ONE underlying idea reworded 100 ways (the 100 problem-aware run all = „frozen forehead + others notice"). That is NOT diversity. Headlines must span a MATRIX: **DESIRE (LF8, PRIMARY axis) × ANGLE (the botox vehicle) × FORMULA (structure)**. Each headline = a DIFFERENT deep desire, not a synonym.

PRIMARY AXIS , LF8 desires (Whitman, Doc 02 §7.0) , the DRIVER of want; the botox angle is only the vehicle that delivers the desire (Schwartz: channel existing mass desire). The 8: 1 Survival/health/vitality (look alive, not waxy) · 2 Food/enjoyment (the social table, laughing with friends) · 3 Freedom from fear/pain/danger (the needle, side effects, irreversibility) · 4 Sexual companionship/attraction/romance (desirable; partner can't read your face) · 5 Comfortable living/money/convenience (no clinic every 3 months, the cost, the easy daily way) · 6 To be superior/status (age better than peers, not look „done") · 7 Care of loved ones (grandchildren, the face they remember) · 8 Social approval (what others say/judge, „did she have work done"). Most potent for this market: LF3, LF4, LF6, LF7, LF8 (LF1/LF5 strong too; LF2 weakest). Distribute a batch across the 8 desires FIRST, then pick the botox angle + formula. Reject a batch that clusters on one desire even if the words/angles differ.
ANGLE axis (the distinct truths about botox , rotate, max ~2 per angle per batch):
1 dependency/cycle · 2 cost-over-time · 3 rebound-when-you-stop · 4 needle-doesn't-fix-the-cause(depth) · 5 side-effects/migration(droopy brow) · 6 mismatch (smooth forehead vs old neck/hands/lower face) · 7 muscle-atrophy/sagging cheeks · 8 the recognizable „done" look · 9 loss-of-identity/чуждо лице · 10 frozen-expression (USE SPARINGLY, max 1-2) · 11 graceful vs сковано (aspiration) · 12 future-self/at-65/legacy · 13 industry-lie/scope-creep/upsell · 14 „smooth = young" myth-bust · 15 the alternative (daily care vs needle) · 16 proof/social (switchers, reviews) · 17 tired-despite-it · 18 relationship-cost.
FORMULA axis: question · reason-why · number/listicle · secret · reframe/contra-intuitive · two-state contrast · lived-moment · warning · testimonial · myth-bust · how-to · „before you…".
RULE: before any headline batch, distribute across the ANGLE axis first (cover many angles), THEN vary formula. Reject a batch that clusters on one angle even if the words differ.

## CLIENT-APPROVED MODELS (the bar , match this)
- „Една жена може да даде цяло състояние за лицето си и пак да изглежда по-уморена от приятелка, която не е давала нищо" (unaware, paradox)
- „Все повече жени над 50 заменят инжекцията на три месеца с нещо, което слагат сами вкъщи за минута" (solution-aware)
- „Има разлика между да замразиш мускула и да подхраниш кожата. Едното спира лицето, другото го връща" (solution-aware contrast)
- „Първата инжекция е заради бръчките. Десетата, защото без нея лицето изглежда по-зле" (problem/awareness fear, lands)
- „Защо да плащаш на всеки три месеца за нещо, което чезне, щом има грижа, която работи всеки ден" (solution-aware value)
